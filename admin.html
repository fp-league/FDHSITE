Monthly stats upload

Upload a CSV each month to bulk-update everyone's stats and points at once instead of editing drivers one by one. First row must be a header row with these exact columns (in any order): callsign, races, wins, podiums, poles, wcc, wdc, power_points. power_points is optional — leave it out (or leave a cell blank) to leave that driver's points untouched. Matching is by callsign, so make sure the spelling matches exactly what's on the site. Rows uploaded here go straight to "approved" status — no separate review step.
